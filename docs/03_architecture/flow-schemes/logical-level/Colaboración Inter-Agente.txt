@startuml
title Protocolo de Colaboración Estructurada Inter-Agente

participant "Agente A (Origen)" as AA
participant "Gateway / Auditor" as GW
participant "Agente B (Destino)" as AB
participant "Context Store" as CS

AA -> GW: **support_request**\n{target: "Finance", question: "X"}
activate GW

GW -> GW: Validar Presupuesto y Ciclo Circular
GW -> CS: Obtener Contexto de Verdad (M_int)

GW -> AB: **Solicitud Estructurada**\n{payload: "X", context_ref: "v1.2"}
activate AB

AB -> AB: Procesar Factores Financieros
AB -> GW: **partial_answer / facts**\n{artifact_type: "FACT", data: "..."}
deactivate AB

GW -> GW: Validar consistencia del Artefacto
GW -> AA: **Entrega de Evidencia**\n{source: "Finance", artifact_id: "uuid"}
activate AA

AA -> AA: Refinar análisis Legal
AA -> GW: **artifact_final** (Legal)
deactivate AA

deactivate GW
@enduml
